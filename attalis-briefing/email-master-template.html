Lead Story · A$3.4bn
Jardine Matheson buys I-MED Radiology from Permira
Jardines is acquiring 100% of I-MED from Permira and other shareholders, including I-MED’s minority stake in Harrison.ai. The deal values the diagnostic imaging platform at roughly A$3.4bn enterprise value and about 11.5x FY26 forecast adjusted EBITDA excluding Harrison.ai.
